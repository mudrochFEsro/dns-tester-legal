DNS/HTTP Tester

Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-04-20

DNS/HTTP Tester ("the App") is a network diagnostic tool. We take your privacy seriously.

Data Collection

The App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All operations run locally on your device.

Local Storage

Query history (up to 50 entries) is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time.

Third-Party Services

The App makes requests to public DNS-over-HTTPS resolvers:

  • Cloudflare (1.1.1.1)
  • Google (8.8.8.8)
  • Quad9 (9.9.9.9)
  • Mozilla
  • Wikimedia

For SSL checks: crt.sh. For WHOIS: rdap.org and api.whois.vu. Only the domain name is sent — no personal identifiers.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or tracking.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
